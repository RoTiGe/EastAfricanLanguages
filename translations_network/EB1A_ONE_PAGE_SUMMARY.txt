INNOVATION HIGHLIGHTS
================================================================================

WHAT MAKES THIS UNIQUE:

Traditional Systems:          My System:
❌ "Good morning" = one       ✅ "Good morning" = 13+ variations
   translation                   (stranger, friend, boss, child, etc.)
❌ No context                 ✅ Multi-dimensional context
❌ Grammar focus              ✅ Social appropriateness focus
❌ Cultural mistakes          ✅ Mistake prevention

EXAMPLE:
"Good morning" in Amharic varies by who you're talking to:
- Stranger: "እንደምን አደሩ" (very formal)
- Friend: "እንደምን አደርክ/ሽ!" (casual)
- Child: "እንደምን አደርክ ፍቅሬ" (affectionate)

My system teaches ALL variations with usage and cultural notes.
